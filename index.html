Bride & GroomOur Story

Mark and Stacy met during a production of the Kaufman and Hart classic play, “You Can’t Take it With You” with Moonstruck Theater Company in 2007. (Mark played Stacy’s dad - and yes, they know that’s weird). After their first show together, the two remained “just friends” until the Spring of 2008. They had almost lost touch when Mark messaged Stacy out of the blue and asked her to grab dinner and talk shop about his next crazy venture - directing a musical with Moonstruck that upcoming summer all while finishing up his master’s degree at UMass Lowell! Was it meant to be a date? Mark and Stacy are not even sure to this day. 8 years, 10+ shows, 4 apartments, and 2 dogs later, they’re best friends, crazy in love and getting married!

Stacy Kadesch

Stacy grew up in Central Massachusetts with her mom Sharon and her little brother, Jimmy. She grew up loving the arts, specifically theater and dance. It was no surprise to her family that she attended music school (The Hartt School, University of Hartford), where she graduated with a BA in Arts Management, Business. She works in the tech sector and specializes in workplace happiness and culture.

Mark Sherman

Mark grew up in Worcester with his younger siblings Rachel and Michael. Their parents Karen and Barry have been hugely supportive of everything they tried, and there was never a dull moment. Among many interests, including theater and cycling, Mark was always fond of science projects, and now does them professionally as a PhD candidate in computer science.
